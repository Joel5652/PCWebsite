SUPPORT

We're here to help

We offer full support from the momment you buy our products. We will provide support with any problems related to the original hardware or operating system of the product.

All support available is online via email or phone call due to COVID restrictions. If you would like to get in contact or enquire about a refund or repair please do so via the contact page.

Refund policy

We offer full refunds and repairs for up to 28 days after purchase if the product is faulty or not in complete working order . In addition, if the product is faulty or damaged upon arrival then please contact us as soon as possible for a replacement.

We will accept no refund enquires from you if the date has exceeded the 28 day limit or if the product has been damaged directly by the buyer.
